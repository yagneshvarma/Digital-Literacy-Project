DESCRIPTION :

I designed my digital literacy infographic using Canva because it’s really easy to use and helps create neat visuals quickly.

In the infographic, I talked about what digital literacy is and why it matters for students today. 
I covered areas like digital tools, online safety, building a good online presence, and proper email habits.
I kept the points short and simple so it’s easy for anyone to read and understand.

I also included examples of tools students use every day, like Google Docs, Gmail, and GitHub. Along with that, I explained how to stay safe online by creating strong passwords and being careful with unknown links.
I added some tips about keeping your online profile professional and writing emails in a respectful way.

While working on this, the hardest part was fitting everything into one page without making it look too crowded.
But I enjoyed choosing colors, icons, and organizing the layout.
Overall, it was a fun activity and helped me learn more about digital literacy while also improving my creativity.
